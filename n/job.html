Position: Web Developer

Job Description

  • Designing, coding, and modifying websites from layout to function according to project specifications.
  • Creating visually appealing web pages or interfaces.
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Staying updated on emerging technologies and industry trends and incorporating them into projects.
  • Testing and debugging websites for functionality, performance, and responsiveness.
  • Optimizing websites for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Ensuring web design is optimized for mobile and other devices.
  • Assisting in the development of applications and features that will be put on the website.
  • Coordinating with marketing and design teams to ensure cohesive branding and messaging on the website.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • Proven working experience in web programming.
  • Strong understanding of UI, cross-browser compatibility, general web functions, and standards.
  • Excellent knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and related web technologies.
  • Experience with responsive and adaptive design.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet tight deadlines.
  • Good communication skills and ability to work in a team.
  • Experience with graphic design applications such as Adobe Photoshop or Sketch is a plus.
